How it works

The Havona platform is a data analytics and business process management platform that empowers enterprises and SMEs with transparency and trust. With Havona, you can reduce the time-to-market for your products, promote transparency in your business operations, and streamline your business processes.

With Havona, it's different. You own your data and can choose who has access to it – improving efficiency and reducing transactional risk eliminating the pain point in global trade finance for SMEs.

What would happen if your business data were combined with data from your customers, suppliers, partners, and competitors with assurances that private transaction data will not be viewed or misused? The Havona Platform is the first to do just that. It does this by providing an efficient and secure way to collect data, visualize it, and then transform it into insights for you to act. This allows you to take on new challenges with agility and efficiency.
