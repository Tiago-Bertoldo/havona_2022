Havona is the world's first cloud native post-trade platform giving you full control of your global operations, eliminating pain points in your transactions.

Problems

Risk

Document fraud, as well payment scams with illegally modified documents. So much potential for headaches.

Transparency

What guarantee do you have that shipment is really on its way? Can you track everything related to your shipment in real-time? Can you track every single change?

Cost

Documents issuance, documents exchange, and documents check, coupled with potential cost of demurrage when documents are incomplete and/or delayed.

Email

Hundreds if not thousands of emails for one single transaction. Coupled with risk of information phishing.

Trust

Do you trust 100% the counterpart? Can you be sure everything will happen as it should?

Paper-based processes

Sluggish, complicated, expensive with so much room for errors.
