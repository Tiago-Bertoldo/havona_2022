How are we?
Two discussions around a coffee was all it took us to realize that not only our ideas were aligned and but our vision was game changing material. We had the power to change things. What the industry needed was not an evolution but required a revolution. Revolution in the way we trade, revolution in the way we exchange data across the globe, revolution in how we use data analytics and technology to provide a better, safer, and more efficient global environment in trading any commodities worldwide. In our experience, most successful businesses find their inspiration from an idea that they've given considerable time to developing. We are no different. However, one of the most important decisions we ever made as a team, was the decision to launch Havona. Back in 2016, our avant-gardist ideas, coupled with blockchain technology as its very early adoption, faced lots of walls, and instead of stopping us, it motivated us to work harder. Following three years of research and planning, we're now ready to set out on the next step: revolutionized post-trade processes We live on achieving our vision for people with an ambition, a commitment to excellence and a sense of responsibility to leave things better than they found them. We work hard every day so that not just but all consumers will have access to terrific products at smart prices, always striving for 'leaving no one behind' by building bridges along the way so that those who do not yet have direct access can cross barriers and get where we are going together as fast as possible! We could not be where we are today without early believers in our ideas, partners that challenged us.
